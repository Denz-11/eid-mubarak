✨ Eid ul Adha Mubarak ✨

Assalamu Alaikum, Dear Guest!

🌙 May the blessings of this sacred day fill your heart with joy, peace, and divine light. 🌙

🕋

The Spirit of Sacrifice

Eid ul Adha commemorates Prophet Ibrahim's (AS) unwavering faith and his readiness to sacrifice his beloved son for Allah.

💫

Faith & Obedience

True sacrifice is surrendering what we hold dearest out of love and devotion to the Almighty, trusting His wisdom.

🤲

Sharing Blessings

The Qurbani meat is shared with family, friends, and the less fortunate — a beautiful act of generosity and empathy.

🌟

Gratitude & Reflection

This Eid invites us to pause, count our blessings, and renew our commitment to righteousness and gratitude.

🕌

Unity & Togetherness

From the congregational prayer to festive gatherings, this blessed day unites the global Ummah in love and solidarity.

🌿

Renewal & Forgiveness

Let us cleanse our hearts, seek forgiveness, and embrace a fresh beginning filled with mercy, hope, and compassion.
